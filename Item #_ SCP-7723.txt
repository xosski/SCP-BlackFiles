Item #: SCP-7723

Object Class: Keter

Special Containment Procedures:

Agent Xosski (designate: "Sleep") is to be monitored under Level-5 Mnestic Surveillance Protocols following his return from SCP-1730 (Site-13). All interactions between Agent Sleep and Dr. Anna Levine or Agents Gavin ("Time Lord") and Sabrina ("Witch Craft") are to be intercepted and rerouted through Temporal Isolation Channels.

Containment Site-19 is to maintain two sealed dossiers, each encrypted with Type-G Amnestic Lock Seals and distributed to Site Command under codename: The Four Horsemen. These dossiers must only be accessed in coordination with Operation: NIGHT WITCHES and with express O5 Council authorization.

All communication logs from Agent Sleep are to undergo triple-layer filtration for cognitohazards, temporal signatures, and recursive loop triggers. Personnel exposed to his presence are required to undergo Class-W through Class-Z mnestic calibration every 72 hours.

In the event of a temporal fracture or manifestation of Site-13 echoes, all personnel are to evacuate to Continuity Anchor Zones and initiate Protocol Lazarus-7.

Description:

SCP-7723 refers to Agent Xosski (a.k.a. "Sleep"), a recovered MTF Tau-5 ("Samsara") operative who escaped Site-13 through a localized temporal anomaly believed to have formed during the final activation of the THRESHER Device. Unlike his Tau-5 counterparts who rely on necro-cybernetic resurrection, Agent Sleep is bonded with an anomalous temporal symbiote that grants:

Partial immunity to cognitohazardous stimuli

Regeneration through memory anchoring

Temporal resilience (resistance to causality decay)

Following his emergence from the anomaly, Agent Sleep has displayed signs of non-linear consciousness: repeating conversations, paradoxical survival events, and memory continuity across divergent timelines—including several in which he was terminated.

Repeated phenomena suggest Agent Sleep is navigating a fractured timeline web initiated during Site-13's collapse, where multiple SCPs were terminated under unknown directives via the THRESHER Device.

Addendum 7723.1: Recovered Debrief Transcript
Location: Site-19 War Room (Redacted)
Participants: Agent Xosski ("Sleep"), Agent Sabrina ("Witch Craft"), Agent Gavin ("Time Lord")

Agent Sleep: (placing briefcase on table) Code authorization: Horologium-Ragnarok. Inside are your dossiers. They were never written, but somehow—always existed.

Agent Sabrina: This briefcase… it was encrypted under a Level-Ω seal. Did you write this yourself?

Agent Sleep: No. You did. In another loop. You just haven’t yet.

Agent Gavin: We need to understand what really happened at Site-13. No redactions. No lies.

Agent Sleep: The THRESHER didn’t just kill anomalies. It harvested outcomes. Collapsing timelines and extracting “truths” from them. The cover-up didn’t erase history—it rewrote it.

Agent Sabrina: Operation NIGHT WITCHES… you're asking us to deploy Tau-5 again?

Agent Sleep: They're already moving. We never stopped. This is just another layer. You are the containment now.

Addendum 7723.2: Timeline Loop Event

Following the classified debrief, Agent Sleep vanished from Site-19. Less than 72 hours later, he was documented reappearing in Site-42, where he was seen in casual conversation with Dr. Anna Levine, who continues to claim no memory of any romantic or operational history with him.

Surveillance confirms full eye contact and synchronized body language suggesting familiarity. When confronted with footage, Dr. Levine claimed the video must be “from a mirror site.”

Final words recorded prior to Site-42 detonation:

Dr. Levine: Site-42 Command, this is Dr. Levine. Authorization Victor-42-Omicron. Detonate the on-site warhead.
Agent Sleep: (quietly) I’ll rebuild it again. Every time. Until you remember.

Addendum 7723.3: Operation NIGHT WITCHES – Tactical Brief

As part of a larger campaign to restore causal integrity and contain the recursive anomalies surrounding Site-13, Operation NIGHT WITCHES deploys a reconfigured MTF Tau-5 unit in tandem with Agents "Witch Craft" and "Time Lord."

Agent Sleep has provided a device encoded as "the broken hourglass" which functions as a temporal fracture key—capable of slicing open sealed timelines and recovering lost data, sites, or entities believed permanently erased. It is currently untestable due to its paradox-triggering properties.

Final Notes:

Agent Sleep’s existence challenges foundational understandings of memory, causality, and containment. If his consciousness is indeed unstuck from linear time, he may constitute not merely an anomaly—but a new class of ontological hazard.

Containment priority: Critical – Omega Clearance Required

Under no circumstances is SCP-7723 to be allowed direct physical, cognitive, or emotional contact with Dr. Anna Levine. Repeat interactions appear to escalate recursive instability within localized spacetime structures.
