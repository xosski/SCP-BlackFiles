Item #: SCP-0000
Object Class: Apollyon (Provisional)
Special Containment Procedures:
Due to the nature of SCP-0000 and its systemic impact on both biological and causal structures, traditional containment procedures are currently ineffective. All known containment protocols have been reabsorbed or overwritten by SCP-0000’s recursive correction field.

Agent Xosski (callsign “Zero”) remains the only confirmed unaffected operative within the affected reality layer. All communication relays within his operational field are to be routed directly to Overwatch Command with auto-redaction enabled after 16 seconds of transmission.

Experimental memetic reboot agents (Ω-Realigners) may be deployed under his discretion. Lethal memetic agents have proven ineffective. Emotional anchor phrases are now preferred over cognitive decouplers.

Public explanation of the marine event is being maintained under the cover story of “deep-sea seismic carbon dispersal testing.” Civilian observations are to be disregarded unless they contain linguistic anomalies.

Description:
SCP-0000 refers to a reality-degrading cognitive collapse phenomenon triggered by the release of an unidentified chemical compound into the Pacific Ocean by a covert governmental organization under the guise of a “carbon-reducing initiative.”

Following dispersal, marine life across a 600-mile radius ceased all visible biological function. Not death — but a suspension of life. No decay. No respiration. No electrical signaling. Just stillness.

Temporal anomalies emerged almost immediately:

Time dilation across affected zones

Causal misalignment (events failing to trigger expected consequences)

Redundancy loops in both language and behavior

Human populations within the radius began exhibiting behavior described as:

Linguistic redundancy (repeating words, questions, or full statements with slight corruption)

Physical recursion (repeating the same bodily movements in short cycles)

Digital synchronization glitches (messages being sent with missing letters, followed by corrected echoes)

Addendum 0000-A: Initial Observations by Agent Xosski (Callsign “Zero”)
Agent Xosski, embedded as a drift-field monitor in coastal ████████, noted the earliest deviations in linguistic coherency. From his field report:

“I asked someone for the time. They said ‘Twelve-twelve’ and blinked twice, like it reset them. Another typed ‘Hey how are yo’ — then a full second later, I got ‘u’ in a separate message. It was like the system had to correct itself to keep pace with causality. Or to hide the break.”

“Everyone’s actions feel malicious, but I don’t think they even know. They’re being rewritten in real time to maintain the illusion of functioning reality. You push — it snaps back. You reboot a mind — the environment snaps it into conformity. This isn’t a virus. It’s a safety protocol of the universe… gone wrong.”
